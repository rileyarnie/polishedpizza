About Us

La Casa is the the spanish term for home, and we are the home for the best pizza in town. We strive to give you the best pizza experience with our signature pizza. The dough is made in house and from scratch. Fresh ingredients are used to deliver the rich home made feel. We were founded in 2019 when our head chef, Arnold Riley, discovered that not pizza made him feel like he was eating from the comfort of his house in the surburbs. His search for the pizza restaurant with the perfect balance of a great menu and ambience was futile. This prompted him to start his own pizza place, we started with one local shop and this grew to a whole chain equipped with delivery services to ensure the service is accessible to as many people as possible.


We have built our company on 3 major values.

Experience

We are pushed to give each and every customer an amazing experience to ensure they have the time of their lives whether they decide to eat in house or dial a delivery.

Love

To create the great family feel and the atmosphere of being at home, we ensure that our pizza is made with lots of love and the customers are treated with love and respect.

Service Delivery

Here at La case, we ensure everyone gets value for their money.We ensure this by making sure our orders and deliveries are completed as fast as possible without lowering the quality standards

OUR MENU

Vegeterian Pizza

This pizza is made with the combination of delicious vegetables like broccoli, onion, capsicum, carrot, mushroom and cauliflower along with tomatoes, pizza sauce, and goat cheese.

Pepperoni Pizza

Our Traditional Pizza Sauce, 100% Whole-Milk Mozzarella, Pepperoni, Mozzarella & Cheddar Mix. This classic pizza is guaranteed to leave you craving more.

Hawaiian Pizza

Our hawaiian pizza is a pizza topped with tomato sauce, cheese, pineapple, and back bacon or ham. This pizza is enured to give you mad tropical vibes after every bite.

Chicken Tikka

Signature Tikka Sauce, Tikka Marinated Chicken, Red Onions, Green Pepper, Tomatoes, Fresh Corriander, 100% whole-milk Mozzarella.

Meat Deluxe

This meaty sensation is our most popular pizza. You will be fully satisfied with our meat deluxe pizza made with our traditional Pizza Sauce, 100% Whole-Milk Mozzarella,Smoked Turkey, Salami, Pepperoni, Sausage, Ground Beef.

Gourmet Classic Italian

Our Creamy Garlic sauce, Pepperoni, Sausage, Fresh Mushrooms, Roma Tomatoes, Green Onions, Mozzarella & Cheddar Mix, Parmesan, Zesty Herbs.

Gourmet Chicken Garlic

Made with our Creamy Garlic Sauce, Grilled Chicken, Roma Tomatoes, Green Onions, Mozzarella & Cheddar Mix, Parmesan, Zesty Herbs. this pizza will leave your mouth exploding with taste.

Margherita

Made with our signature Pizza Sauce, 100% Whole-Milk Mozzarella, Topped with a Mozzarella & Cheddar Mix, this cream-filled pizza will have you wanting more.

House Special

This is our very own secret recipe made with our traditional Pizza Sauce, 100% Whole-Milk Mozzarella, Pepperoni, Sausage, Ground Beef, Fresh Mushrooms, Onion Mix, Green Peppers, Black Olives.

Gourmet Veggie

Made from our Creamy Garlic sauce, Fresh Spinach, Zucchini, Fresh Mushrooms, Artichoke Hearts, Roma Tomatoes, Onion Mix, Mozzarella & Cheddar Mix, Parmesan, Zesty Herbs.

Sweet Potato Walnuts Pizza

This unique pizza provides an option for our vegan customers to note get left out of the experience we strive to give everyone. The pizza is topped up with our signature sauce, sweet potatoes, stilton and walnuts. This gives a unique blend of taste in the mouth.

Lamb Pizza

This pizza is served with or without cheese, and often with the cheese underneath the sauce to prevent the pie from becoming soggy. This coupled with the lamb shows off our staff's elite culinary skills.
